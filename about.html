About Us

Welcome to Movie Magic! We are dedicated to providing you with the best movie recommendations and genre explorations. Our community is passionate about movies and loves to share their thoughts and reviews. Join us and be a part of the Movie Magic family!

Our team is made up of movie enthusiasts who are always on the lookout for the latest trends and classics in the film industry. We strive to create a platform where movie lovers can come together to discuss, share, and enjoy all things related to movies.

This project is brought to you by a team of four computer engineering students from Government Engineering College - Rajkot, under Gujarat Technological University:
